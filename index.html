ABOUT US

FRIGHTFEST 2025: Andhara pradesh's grandest literary and cultural spectacle returns with an eerie twist—"Mythica: Shadows of the Past and Magic of the Future." From 7th to 9th February, 2025, Rajiv gandhi University of Knowledge Technologuies,Nuzvid, becomes a portal to a realm where ancient secrets collide with forbidden magic. Unravel the mysteries of long-forgotten legends, venture into the shadows of the unknown, and awaken the arcane forces that blur the line between past and future. Prepare for a spine-chilling journey through myth and mystery—where every whisper hides a story, and every shadow holds a secret. Dare to join us ?
